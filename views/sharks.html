Info about Maine Coon cats

Maine Coon cats, or simply Maine cats, are said to have originated from the name "Maine Coon" from the cold state of Maine in the New England region of the United States.The Maine Coon cat we know today as the “gentle giant”

Maine coon cats are also known as “the clowns of the cat world” because of their unique characteristics and personalities.Maine Coon cats especially love to snuggle up with their loved ones to feel the warm, loving hugs.
