Cat Health Guide

Understanding and maintaining your cat's health

Emergency Warning Signs

Seek immediate veterinary care if your cat shows any of these symptoms:

  • Difficulty breathing or rapid breathing
  • Collapse or inability to stand
  • Seizures
  • Unconsciousness
  • Severe bleeding

Common Health Issues

Urinary Issues

Common in cats, especially males. Watch for:

  • Frequent urination
  • Straining to urinate
  • Blood in urine
  • Urinating outside litter box

Dental Disease

Affects most cats over 3 years. Signs include:

  • Bad breath
  • Difficulty eating
  • Red or swollen gums
  • Loose or missing teeth

Parasites

Common external and internal parasites:

  • Fleas and ticks
  • Worms
  • Ear mites
  • Skin irritation

Preventive Care

Regular Check-ups

Annual veterinary visits are essential for:

  • Vaccinations
  • Physical examination
  • Blood work
  • Dental check

Vaccinations

Core vaccines for cats include:

  • FVRCP (Feline Viral Rhinotracheitis)
  • Rabies
  • Feline Leukemia (FeLV)
  • FIV (Feline Immunodeficiency Virus)
